Explore The Canadian Rockies


Rating


Adventure and excitement await you in Canada’s Rocky Mountains. Here in the land of snow-capped mountains and crystal clear alpine lakes you’ll find one of the most beautiful, unspoiled vacation spots you can imagine.

Amid scenery that will take your breath away, the Rocky Mountains are a year-round adventure playground, offering a wealth of winter and summer activities.

Maligne Lake Cruise

Experience the world-famous Spirit Island located on Maligne Lake aboard a 90 minute interpretive boat cruise.

Glacier Adventure

Massive Ice Explorers take passengers on the surface of the Athabasca Glacier. Followed by a jaw-dropping walk along the cliff-edge, glass-floored Glacier Skywalk.

Icefields Discovery

Walk on the Athabasca Glacier, and experience views from the awe-inspiring Glacier Skywalk.

Banff Lake Cruise

This magnificent interpretive lake cruise of Minnewanka and the scenic Canadian Rockies is 1 hour long and provides unique photo opportunities.

Taste and Explore

Explore the Banff and the Yoho area on this small luxury tour. Experience authentic Rocky Mountain cuisine for lunch and the historical Deer Lodge.

Elk + Avenue Hotel

The Elk + Avenue Hotel invites you to experience warm hospitality, exceptional comfort and breath-taking mountaintop views from a prized location in downtown Banff.

Exploring Jasper

Explore the largest national park in the Rockies. This scenic tour visits Jasper's beautiful sights, including Maligne Lake and the town of Jasper.

Snowmobiling Paradise Basin

From soft white powder bowls to abandoned mines, this winter adventure zips you through boreal forests and Canadian Rockies back-country on snowmobile.

Winter Wildlife Tour

An abundance of wildlife can be found in Jasper National Park, even in the winter. Travel with a guide, searching the frozen landscape for elk, deer, moose, wolves, and sheep.

Johnson Canyon Icewalk

Discover magnificent icefalls as you traverse the steel catwalks of Johnson Canyon for spectaular views of the canyon below. This is must-do winter activity is perfect for all ages.
