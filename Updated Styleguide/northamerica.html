Calgary Stampede

On this Calgary Stampede tour, enter a world of Wild West excitement as you sample chuckwagon fare and watch rodeo action in Calgary. Two days admission to the Calgary Stampede; heart-stopping chuckwagon races; an outdoor stage show with singers, dancers, and musicians; a souvenir program; and more are included in your vacation. Globus has also arranged a behind-the-scenes visit to an authentic First Nations village, where you’ll learn more about the Native people. Bring your cowboy hat and consider combining this with one of our Canadian Rockies tours.

Whistler

Reach the alpine on tours by helicopter, all-terrain vehicle or snowcat, or ride the PEAK 2 PEAK Gondola for 360-degree views and access to 19 alpine trails. Take a sightseeing train tour aboard the Rocky Mountaineer Whistler Sea to Sky Climb train, or join a bus tour for spectacular ocean and mountain views.

Explore the Rocky's

The Rocky Mountains of Canada are a vast land – filled with inspiring scenery, wildlife, a multitude of recreational activities for all ages and a lot of unique opportunities that only the people who live in this area know about.
